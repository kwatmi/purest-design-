Founding Philosophy

The Education Mandate of Living Faith Church Worldwide was delivered on 12th May,1998 to God’s Servant, Bishop (Dr.) David Oyedepo, the visionary leader and Apostle over the Living Faith Church, Worldwide. According to him, there was a marching order of the church into the establishment of educational institutions from God as follows: ‘As the light of the world, go and set the pace, go and blaze the trail, go and show them the way to do it’
